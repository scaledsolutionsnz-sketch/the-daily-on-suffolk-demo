Our place

A café built for the regulars.

The Daily on Suffolk is the kind of spot you fall into the habit of. Good coffee made by people who care, food that tastes like someone cooked it, and a welcome that doesn't change whether it's your first visit or your fiftieth.

We keep the menu tight and seasonal, lean on local suppliers, and bake what we can in-house. Come for the coffee, stay for a feed, bring the dog to the table outside.

  • Locally owned and run, right here in the neighbourhood
  • Coffee dialled in fresh and made by friendly baristas
  • Dog-friendly outdoor tables and room for the pram
