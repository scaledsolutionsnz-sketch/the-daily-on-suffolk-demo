Privacy Policy

How we look after the personal information you share with The Daily on Suffolk.

Last updated: this is placeholder content for the demo and should be reviewed before going live.

1. Who we are

The Daily on Suffolk is a café and eatery in Christchurch, New Zealand. We respect your privacy and handle personal information in line with the Privacy Act 2020.

2. What we collect

We only collect what we need to respond to you. When you use our enquiry or feedback forms, that's typically your name, email, phone number (if you give it) and the message you send. We don't ask for payment details through this website.

3. How we use it

  • To reply to your enquiry, catering request or booking
  • To follow up on feedback and improve what we do
  • To keep records of orders and arrangements you make with us

4. Forms and email

Our website forms are delivered to us by email through a third-party form service (FormSubmit). Your message is sent to our inbox and is subject to that provider's handling in transit. We don't sell or rent your information to anyone.

5. Sharing

We don't share your personal information with third parties except where it's needed to provide a service you've asked for, or where we're required to by law.

6. Cookies and analytics

This website uses minimal cookies. If we add analytics in future, it will be to understand general site usage in aggregate, not to identify you personally.

7. Your rights

You can ask to see the personal information we hold about you and request that we correct it. Just get in touch using the details below.

8. Contact

For any privacy question, email thedaily.onsuffolk@gmail.com or call 027 449 0305.
